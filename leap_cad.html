Printer:

We use Ender 3 S1 Pro.
Any deposition-based printer will work, but direct drive such as S1 is required for flexible filament.

Software:

I recommend Cura 5, choose your correct printer. With Cura and the regular quality setting, all parts can be printed in under 24 hours.
See here for sample .3mf files.

Settings (PLA):
  • Palm: 60% Infill, supports.
  • Rest: 100% Infill, no support.
  • 200C Nozzle and 60C Bed
    • Settings (Ninjaflex Edge for Optional Soft Fingertips):
  • 20% Infill no support.
  • 240C Nozzle and 60C Bed
